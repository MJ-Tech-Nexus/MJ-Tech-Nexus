## Executive Summary

Enterprise automation architect with **12+ years in IT**, specializing in the convergence of **Agentic AI, Generative AI, and Intelligent Automation**. I design and lead platforms where AI agents, LLM applications, and UiPath robots operate as one governed system — from process discovery through production at enterprise scale.

- 🧠 **Agentic AI** — multi-agent orchestration, tool-use, memory, human-in-the-loop design
- ⚙️ **Intelligent Automation** — UiPath RPA at scale: architecture, CoE standards, reusable frameworks
- 📚 **LLM Applications & RAG** — grounded retrieval, evaluation pipelines, prompt engineering
- 🔍 **Process Mining** — data-driven discovery feeding the automation & AI pipeline
- ☁️ **Cloud AI Solutions** — Azure OpenAI, AWS, containerized AI services
- 🏛️ **Enterprise Architecture** — governance, security, observability, and cost control for AI estates
